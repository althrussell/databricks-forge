# Why Forge?

## Turn Databricks metadata into business value in days, not quarters

You already invested in Databricks. Forge helps you get more value from that investment faster by turning your Unity Catalog metadata into a prioritized, execution-ready roadmap of analytics and AI opportunities -- complete with financial quantification, stakeholder mapping, and deployable artifacts.

In one flow, your team can discover high-impact use cases, quantify their business value, align them to strategy, and deploy what matters first, without weeks of workshops or manual discovery.

## Built for Databricks Customers

### Works with your platform, not around it

Forge is designed for teams already running on Databricks:

- Uses your **Unity Catalog** metadata as the source of truth.
- Runs SQL on your **SQL Warehouse**.
- Uses your **Model Serving** endpoints for AI generation.
- Stores state in **Lakebase** (auto-provisioned Postgres).
- Deploys as a **Databricks App** in your workspace.

No new platform to adopt. No extra infrastructure to maintain.

### Enterprise-ready trust and governance

- **Metadata-first by default:** Schema and table metadata only, unless you explicitly enable sampling.
- **Workspace-native processing:** Data remains in your Databricks environment.
- **Transparent outputs:** AI-generated recommendations are traceable and reviewable.

## Key Capabilities

### Prioritized Use Case Catalog

A single, scored backlog your business and data teams can align on quickly. Every use case includes domain assignment, priority/feasibility/impact scores, and runnable SQL.

### Business Value Intelligence

Go beyond discovery with financially-grounded analysis:

- **Financial Quantification** -- dollar-range estimates (low/mid/high) per use case, categorised by cost savings, revenue uplift, risk reduction, or efficiency gain.
- **Implementation Roadmap** -- delivery phases (Quick Wins / Foundation / Transformation) with effort estimates, dependencies, and enablers.
- **Executive Synthesis** -- board-ready key findings, strategic recommendations, and risk callouts.
- **Stakeholder Analysis** -- organisational impact mapping with champion identification and department-level views.
- **Use Case Voting** -- workshop-style prioritisation where team members vote on the highest-value opportunities.
- **Strategy Alignment** -- upload strategy documents and see how discovered use cases align to strategic initiatives, with gap analysis (Supported / Partial / Blocked).

<!-- SCREENSHOT NEEDED: guide-31-roadmap.png -- Implementation Roadmap with delivery timeline chart -->

### Value Tracking and Capture

Track the lifecycle of every use case from discovery to measured value:

- Stage pipeline (Discovered, Planned, In Progress, Delivered, Measured).
- Inline owner editing and stalled indicators (14+ day alerts).
- Value capture records to measure actual delivered impact against estimates.

### Genie Space Management

Deploy production-grade Genie Spaces with deep knowledge stores, then continuously improve them:

- **Multi-pass generation** -- column intelligence, semantic SQL expressions, trusted queries, text instructions, benchmarks, and metric view proposals.
- **Health check scoring** -- 20 deterministic checks across data sources, instructions, semantic richness, and quality assurance with A-F grades.
- **Automated fix workflow** -- targeted fixes mapped to failing health checks, with optimisation review and diff preview before applying.
- **Benchmark evaluation** -- run test questions against deployed spaces, label results, and generate targeted improvements.
- **Import external spaces** -- paste JSON from any Genie Space for health checking and improvement.
- **Inline editing** -- rename measures, edit instructions, remove expressions before deploying.
